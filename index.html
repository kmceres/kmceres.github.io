About me

I'm a combined DVM/PhD candidate in the Grohn laboratory at Cornell University. My PhD focus is in population medicine and epidemiology with minors in applied statistics and genomics. I'm interested in blending population genomics and epidemiological methods to understand infectious disease population dynamics.
