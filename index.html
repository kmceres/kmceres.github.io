About me

I'm a combined DVM/PhD candidate in the Gröhn lab at Cornell University. My PhD focus is in population medicine and epidemiology with minors in applied statistics and genomics. I'm interested in blending computational genomics and epidemiological methods to understand infectious disease population dynamics.
